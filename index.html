Project Overview

The Chopping Block is the Spring 2026 capstone project for 404-Found, built from scratch as a health equity mobile application for students and younger kids. It uses play, repetition, unlocks, and progression to make healthy eating education more engaging and easier to retain.

Engine Unity 6 with the Universal Render Pipeline
Language C# with Unity scene and manager architecture
Target Kids and students learning healthy food recognition
Release Stage Beta release portfolio snapshot

Core Loop

  • Tap healthy foods during gameplay to score points and earn coins.
  • Unlock more foods through score milestones and progression.
  • Use the Trophy Case to connect gameplay rewards to nutrition learning.
  • Support replay through score chasing, menu flow, tutorial support, and persistent progress.

Beta Scope

  • Classic gameplay mode
  • Score, lives, and game over flow
  • Food unlock progression
  • Trophy Case and fun fact screens
  • Settings, audio, and tutorial support
